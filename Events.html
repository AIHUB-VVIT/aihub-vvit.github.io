Team meetup#03 (13/04/2025): Bhargav Venkat, Mentor of our community, discussed the process involved in establishing a server for our website. He also discussed that setting up challenges for competitive programming or hackathons is actually a career opportunity. He insisted that the students should give high priority in developing problem skills more than learning just some list of tech stacks.

In this online team meetup session Bhargav Venkat, Mentor of our community, explained how the python library 'behave' can actually help communities to develop software collaboratively. He also motivated us to take up challenging projects without any fear.

An introduction to Kaggle website, (i) how to create kaggle account, (ii) browsing the competitions, (iii) joining a competition, and (iv) how to do submission of the finished work into the competitions were explained by Rama Charan, Mentor. The other learning resources like courses and computing facilities like jupyter notebooks available were also discussed.
